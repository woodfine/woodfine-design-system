# Woodfine News Release Standards

This document defines the typographic rules for Woodfine Management Corp. news releases, inherited from the COMM master standards.

## Core Rules
* **Alignment:** Left-aligned (ragged right) only. No justified text.
* **Capitalization:** Title Case for headers. No all-caps for Woodfine Management Corp. or geographic locations.
* **Geography:** Use full spelling (e.g., British Columbia).

## Print Layout Standard
**For Immediate Release**

**[Woodfine Headline in Title Case]**

**Vancouver, British Columbia – [Date] –** Woodfine Management Corp. ("Woodfine")...

## Web Layout Standard (HTML/CSS)
**Woodfine Management Corp. — Press Release**

**[Woodfine Headline in Title Case]**

**For immediate release | Vancouver, British Columbia | [Date]**

Woodfine Management Corp. ("Woodfine")...
